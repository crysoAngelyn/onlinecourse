About Us


Every course on Coursera is taught by top instructors from the world’s best universities and educational institutions. Courses include recorded video lectures, auto-graded and peer-reviewed assignments, and community discussion forums. When you complete a course, you’ll receive a sharable electronic Course Certificate.


Many talents. One vision.


We’re a vibrant, diverse, and big-hearted community of more than 300 employees. We’re not just engineers, designers, marketers, and managers – we’re also DJs, triathletes, published scholars, parents, pet owners, artists, adventurers, and more. We bring our whole selves to work, and we’re united by our belief in the transformative power of learning and our commitment to expanding access to education around the world.


We don’t just accept diversity – we celebrate it, support it, and thrive on it for the benefit of our employees, our partners, our platform, and our community. We are a proud equal  opportunity employer.


Our Achievements


We’re a fast-growing, fast-moving leader in the online education space, with millions of registered learners and thousands of courses on our platform. Our technology pushes boundaries of what’s possible in education, empowering individuals and organizations to learn effectively as they grow and change.


Our enterprise customers include top companies like L’Oreal and BCG. We’re backed by top Silicon Valley investors, and we were named one of the Top 50 Disruptors of 2016 by CNBC. While our progress is big, the opportunity is bigger – the human community is more than 7 billion strong, and we believe that every single life can be transformed by access to the best education.
