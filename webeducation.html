About Us




WebEdu was founded in 2012 by two Stanford Computer Science professors who wanted to share their knowledge and skills with the world.

Professors Daphne Koller and Andrew Ng put their courses online for anyone to take and taught more learners in a few months than they could have in an entire lifetime in the classroom.


Since then, we’ve built a platform where anyone, anywhere can learn and earn credentials from the world’s top universities and education providers.
